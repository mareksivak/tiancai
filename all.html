Sales AI App

Sales app using mental state recognition to help sales reps understand success of their video meetings based on emotions.

Mockup shows a report of the meeting with highlights which we called alerts - moments in the meeting where are significant portions of particular state detected. The goal was to help users review a 60 mins long call in a few seconds.

On Friday, with Carter we looked into Invision prototype which I'd been using to get feedback. Shows list of past meetings, meeting reports (emotional analysis of the meeting; there is a screen sharing during call) and helps to track deals.

Design for meeting report here was iterated based on insights from users - new layout and mental states are visualised using charts
