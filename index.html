Sales AI App

Sales app using mental state recognition to help sales reps understand success of their video meetings based on emotions.

Mockup shows a report of the meeting with highlights which we called alerts - moments in the meeting where significant portions of particular state(s) was detected. The goal was to help users review a 60 mins long call in a few seconds.

On inverviews with users I'd been using an Invision prototype. Shows list of past meetings, meeting reports (emotional analysis of the meeting; there is a screen sharing during call) and helps to track deals.
